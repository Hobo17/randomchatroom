This started as a social experiment as part of a guest lecture at an esteemed institute of learning and will continue as a social experiment.

It was set up as a way for those people in the audience to ask questions who were too shy. This was the beginning of the madness.

If you want to help develop the madness (you don't have to be a coder), join the google group at http://groups.google.com/group/randomchatroom . It will be a fun learning experience at the very least.
